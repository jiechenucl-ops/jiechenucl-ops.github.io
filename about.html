Jie Chen is currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Chemistry at University College London (UCL), supervised by Prof Guanjie He and Professor Ivan Parkin. He obtained his bachelor's and master's degrees in physics from Lanzhou University, and served as the visiting student at Fudan University. His research interests mainly focus on the interface between electrodes and electrolytes in aqueous batteries, as well as aqueous batteries and interdisciplinary research across multiple fields (electrocatalysis, interfacial aqueous chemistry, X-ray computed tomography, machine learning).
