About Me

I am Jie Chen, a PhD student at University College London (UCL). My research integrates electrochemistry, interfacial water chemistry, X-ray computed tomography (CT), and data-driven analysis to understand water-mediated charge transfer and failure mechanisms in aqueous zinc systems.

I enjoy interdisciplinary research combining experiments, imaging, and modelling, with the goal of advancing fundamental understanding and improving reliability in aqueous electrochemical technologies.

Research Interests

  • • Aqueous zinc-ion batteries
  • • Interfacial water chemistry & charge transfer
  • • X-ray computed tomography (CT) & AI segmentation
  • • Separator degradation & dendrite–separator interaction
  • • Water-mediated reaction mechanisms in electrochemical systems
