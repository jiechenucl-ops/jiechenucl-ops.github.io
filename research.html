Research

My research focuses on aqueous zinc batteries, interfacial water chemistry, and X-ray CT/AI-assisted analysis of electrochemical failure mechanisms. I am particularly interested in water-mediated charge storage, separator-centric failure in Zn–V systems, and radical chemistry emerging from ion-induced water homolysis.

Research Themes

  • Aqueous zinc batteries and interfacial water chemistry.
  • Water-mediated charge storage and fast-charging mechanisms.
  • Separator-centric failure in Zn–V and related battery systems.
  • X-ray computed tomography (CT) and data-driven analysis for microstructure and failure.
  • Ion-enhanced water homolysis and radical chemistry in electrochemical environments.
  • Two-dimensional MXene materials and the principle of crystal growth.
